{{< recipe
  title="Grilled Chicken Breast (Coriander/Cinnamon)"
>}}

{{< recipe-ingredients >}}
- Brine chicken breasts (four cups cold water, 1TBS salt, soak chicken
- in it for 1-6 hours).
- rinse chicken.  Rub with:
- 1/2 tsp cayenne
- 1tsp coriander
- 1/2 tsp cinnamon.
- grill 3 min. per side, or until 160 degrees.
