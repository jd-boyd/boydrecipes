{{< recipe
  title="Alfredo's Sauce"
>}}

{{< recipe-ingredients >}}
- 1/4 cup butter
- 1 cup heavy cream
- 2/3 cup parmesan
- 12oz fettucini
- Melt butter
- Add cream.  Bring to boil, reduce to simmer for 5 minutes
- Turn off heat.
- Drain pasta.
{{< /recipe-ingredients >}}

{{< recipe-instructions >}}
- Add parmesan, salt and pepper
- Mix pasta and sauce.  Serve promptly.
{{< /recipe-instructions >}}
