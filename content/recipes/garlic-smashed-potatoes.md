{{< recipe
  title="Garlic Smashed Potatoes￼"
>}}

{{< recipe-ingredients >}}
- 1 lb baby Yukon Gold potatoes
- Kosher salt
- 4 tbsp melted butter
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tsp fresh thyme leaves
- Freshly ground black pepper
- 1/2 c freshly grated Parmesan
{{< /recipe-ingredients >}}

{{< recipe-instructions >}}
- preheat over to 425°F.
- in a large pot, cover potatoes with water and salt
- bring to boil
- simmer until tender (about 15 minutes)
- drain a let cool till easy to touch
- on half sheet pan, toss potatoes with butter, garlic, thyme.
- lightly smash with something hard
- season
- sprinkle with parm
- bake until potatoes are starting to crisp and parmesan is browning (approximately 25 minutes).
{{< /recipe-instructions >}}
