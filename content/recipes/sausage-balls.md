{{< recipe
  title="Sausage Balls"
>}}

{{< recipe-ingredients >}}
- 1 pound hot sausage
- 1 pound cheddar cheese, grated
- 3 cups bisquick
- 1/2 cup milk
- Mix together, don't overmix.
- Drop on cookie sheet with 1 or 3/4 oz. baller (50 to 72 balls).
- Freeze or cook immediately.
- Bake at 350 for 15-25 minutes until GBD, longer if frozen.
{{< /recipe-ingredients >}}
