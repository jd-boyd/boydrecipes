{{< recipe
  title="Crab Rangoon"
>}}

{{< recipe-ingredients >}}
- 8 oz cream cheese, room temp.
- 2 green onions, finely sliced
- Crab, 10 oz can (2 tuna sized cans, or fresh, or other).
- 48 wonton wrappers
{{< /recipe-ingredients >}}

{{< recipe-instructions >}}
- Mix crab, onion, and cream cheese.
- Put 2 tsp in each wonton wrapper, seal with water.
- Fry in oil at 350 ℉
{{< /recipe-instructions >}}
