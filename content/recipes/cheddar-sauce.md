{{< recipe
  title="Cheddar Sauce"
>}}

{{< recipe-ingredients >}}
- Roux:
- 1 tbsp butter, 1 tbsp flour.
- 2 tsp dijon
- 1 cup milk
- thicken 3-5 minutes.
- Melt in 1/3 cup cheddar cheese.  Add salt, pepper.
- Serve over steamed broccoli, etc.
