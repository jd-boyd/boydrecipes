{{< recipe
  title="Gertrudes Chocolate Cake"
>}}

{{< recipe-ingredients >}}
- Preheat over to 400F.
- 4T cocoa
- 2 sticks margin (NOT butter under any circumstance)
- 1C water
- 2C flour
- 2C sugar
- 1/2t baking soda
- 1/2C buttermilk
- 2 eggs
- Bake at 400F for 20 minutes.  Remove cake from oven and apply icing (below).
- Icing:
- 4T cocoa
- 1 stick margerin
- 2-4T milk
- mix in  pound 10x sugar
- Optional:
- sprinkle 1/2C nuts on top.
{{< /recipe-ingredients >}}

{{< recipe-instructions >}}
- mix and pour into 9x13 cake pan
{{< /recipe-instructions >}}
