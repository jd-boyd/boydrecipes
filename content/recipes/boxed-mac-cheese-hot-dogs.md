{{< recipe
  title="Boxed Mac & Cheese + Hot Dogs"
>}}

{{< recipe-ingredients >}}
- 2 boxes Kraft (or similar enough generic, like Wegmans) Mac & Cheese
- 1 package (1 pound) pork/turkey dogs (all beef or beef included aren't as good)
- 3/4 c milk
- 1/2 c butter (1 stick), in approximately 8 chunks
- 2 tsp Mustard powder
{{< /recipe-ingredients >}}

{{< recipe-instructions >}}
- 1. Boil water (about 12 cups).
- 1. Chop hot dogs into ~ 1/2" sections.
- 1. Add pasta from boxes and hot dogs, cook 6 minutes
- 1. Drain pasta and dogs and return to pan, off burner.
- 1. Add butter, mustard, and cheese packages.  Stir until melted.
- 1. Add Milk, stir in.
{{< /recipe-instructions >}}
