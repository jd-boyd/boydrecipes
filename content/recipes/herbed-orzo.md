>}}

{{< recipe-ingredients >}}
- 1 cup orzo3 tbsp chopped fresh herbs2 tsp lemon juice1 tbsp butter
{{< /recipe-ingredients >}}

{{< recipe-instructions >}}
- In a large pot of boiling salted water, cook orzo till al dente.Drain orzo and transfer to a medium bowl.Add fresh herbs.Add butter.Add lemon juice.Toss to combine.Salt and pepper to taste.
{{< /recipe-instructions >}}
