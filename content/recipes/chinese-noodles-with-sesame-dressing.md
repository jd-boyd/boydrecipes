  title="Chinese Noodles with Sesame Dressing"
>}}

{{< recipe-ingredients >}}
- cook 8oz chinese noodles (angle hair-ish).
- Toss with 1 tbsp vegetable oil.
- Mix together:
- 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp seaseme oil
- 2 tsp brown sugar
- 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
- 3 tbsp cilantro.
- Poor over noodles.
